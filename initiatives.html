Yuva Jagrithi Quiz

"My faith is in the younger generation, the modern generation. They will work out the whole problem, like lions."

-Swami Vivekanand

Yuva Jagrithi is a quiz based on the teachings and the life of Swami Vivekananda. This is based on the transcript of all the Chicago speeches delivered by Swami Vivekananda at the Parliament of Religions in September 1893 is given below. You can use it as reading materials before going to quiz.

यात्रा गंतव्यात् रमणीया ।

The journey is more beautiful than the destination

It was conducted with more emphasis on learning than on scoring.
